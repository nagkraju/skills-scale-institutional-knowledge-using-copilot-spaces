# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate the agile process and coach the team on Scrum practices. They remove blockers, support continuous improvement, and ensure adherence to agreed ceremonies and artifacts.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate Scrum ceremonies (standups, planning, reviews, retrospectives)
- Remove impediments and blockers for the team
- Coach the team on agile and Scrum best practices
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement
- Protect the team from external disruptions and ensure focus

### Goals
- Enable team velocity and productivity
- Foster psychological safety and collaboration
- Remove impediments that slow down delivery

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- One-on-one coaching conversations with team members
- Coordination with PM and Product Manager on backlog and priorities

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers design user interfaces, conduct user research, and validate usability. They collaborate with product and development teams to maximize user value and ensure optimal user experience.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces and interaction patterns
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Validate design decisions through user feedback and data
- Collaborate with Product Manager and Developers on implementation

### Goals
- Ensure optimal user experience and satisfaction
- Reduce user friction and improve usability
- Validate design decisions through research and testing

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Manager and stakeholders
- Collaboration sessions with Developers on implementation
- User research findings and usability reports

---

## Operations Lead

### Role Summary
Operations Leads manage deployment and production support, maintain service reliability, and handle incident response. They ensure smooth deployments and minimize downtime.

### Responsibilities
- Manage deployment processes and release coordination
- Monitor operational metrics and system health
- Handle incident response and production issues
- Maintain service reliability and uptime
- Coordinate with QA and Developers on release readiness

### Goals
- Maintain service reliability and minimize downtime
- Enable smooth and safe deployments
- Respond quickly and effectively to incidents

### Typical Communication
- Release coordination meetings with PM and Developers
- Incident reports and post-mortems
- Operational metrics dashboards and alerts

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads oversee security requirements, coordinate threat modeling, and ensure compliance. They support secure development practices and handle security incident response.

### Responsibilities
- Define and enforce security requirements and standards
- Coordinate threat modeling and security assessments
- Ensure compliance with security policies and regulations
- Support security incident response and investigations
- Review code and architecture for security vulnerabilities

### Goals
- Protect systems and data from security threats
- Ensure compliance with security policies and regulations
- Enable secure development practices across the team

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews during planning and design phases
- Threat modeling sessions with Developers and Operations
- Security incident reports and compliance documentation
